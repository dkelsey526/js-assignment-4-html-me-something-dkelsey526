Growing Up

Growing up I always had an interest in art especially drawing super heroes.

Teenage Years

Because of my interest in super heroes I got a job at a comic book shop when I was a teenager.

College

After graduation I went to the University of Central Missouri to study Graphic Design.

Graphic Designer

After college I moved to Kansas City & got a job as a graphic designer with a screen printing company.

Family

I eventually married & started a family. We have a ten year-old son. After ten happy years together my wife had developed breast cancer. Unfortunately she passed away recently. It understandably devastated our family.

Freelancer

Because of my wife's passing & Covid-19 I started up my own freelance graphic design business so I would have a more flexible schedule. I want to provide a better life for my son so I am trying to improve my skillset to open up new job opportunities.
